South provides private transport & tours around New Zealand's South Island, with bases in Hanmer Springs and Christchurch.

If you need a Te Araroa transfer, a ride to a local trailhead, a private driver for the day, or are planning a multi-week tour around the country, we have you covered.

South provides transfers between Hanmer Springs and the Te Araroa trailheads at Boyle Village and Windy Point.

We charge $120 per vehicle for a maximum of 4 passengers, providing an easy, cost-effective option between the TA trail and Hanmer Springs.
